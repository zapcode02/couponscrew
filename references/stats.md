## Affiliate industry context (India)

- **India affiliate industry size (2025):** expected to exceed $800 million USD
- **CPS (Cost Per Sale) pays higher commissions** than CPC or CPA models — better long-term revenue for coupon sites
- **Best performing affiliate categories for coupon sites:** Fashion, Beauty, Food delivery (high frequency + high commission = best combo)
- **Electronics:** low commission rate (1–2%) but massive order values — worth targeting for coupon content even if affiliate earnings are low
- **Fashion is the sweet spot:** high commission (8–12%) + high purchase frequency + strong sale event culture in India

## Savings stack potential (use when explaining layering)

| Layer | Typical saving |
|---|---|
| Sale price discount | 20–70% |
| Coupon code on top | ₹100–₹500 flat or 5–15% extra |
| Bank card offer (HDFC/ICICI/SBI) | 5–10% instant |
| Cashback app (browser extension) | 1–5% cashback |
| **Combined realistic saving** | **35–80% off original price** |

## Bank card offer benchmarks

- **HDFC card offers:** common on Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra — typically 10% instant
- **ICICI card offers:** common on Flipkart, Swiggy — typically 10% instant
- **SBI card offers:** common on Amazon GIF — typically 10% instant
- **Axis card offers:** common on Zomato, travel platforms

## SEO benchmarks for coupon content

- **Coupon pages with 1,500+ words of unique content** are indexable and rankable — under that, consider noindex or consolidation
- **Long-tail keywords with coupon modifiers** ("brand + coupon code", "brand + promo code today") drive highest-intent traffic
- **Hub pages per category** (3,500+ words) is the structure used by RetailMeNot and Honey to rank for thousands of keywords

## How to use these numbers

- **Always specific, never vague.** "10,000+ verified coupons", not "thousands of coupons."
- **Use real rupee amounts for savings.** Not "save more" — say "saves you ₹500 on a ₹3,000 order."
- **For affiliate content:** always mention both the coupon saving AND the affiliate link angle — e.g. "grab the code below and click through to get the deal."
- **Commission rates change** — always verify on the live program page before a post goes live.
- **Platform stats are benchmarks** — frame as "typically" or "up to" when relevant.
